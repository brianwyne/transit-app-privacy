Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This app provides real‑time transit information and operator‑focused tools. To deliver these features, the app may access your device’s location and store certain user‑entered information on your device. This privacy policy explains how that information is used.

1. Location Information

The app may access your device’s approximate or precise location for the following purposes:

  • Finding nearby bus and rail stops
  • Retrieving departure times for stops near your current location
  • Helping identify the bus or rail vehicle serving your trip

Your location is used only in real time and only on your device. It is not stored, logged, analyzed, or transmitted to any server.

2. User‑Entered Information (Stored Locally Only)

The app allows you to enter certain work‑related details, including:

  • Name
  • Badge number
  • Employee ID
  • Pay rate
  • Regular days off
  • Other operator‑specific fields

This information is stored locally on your device and is used only to support app features such as schedule display and operator tools. It is never transmitted, shared, analyzed, or accessible to the developer. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

3. Backups and User‑Initiated Data Export

The app allows you to create a local backup of your operator data and email the backup file to yourself or another destination of your choice.

Important details:

  • Backups are created only on your device
  • Backups are never uploaded automatically
  • The app does not send your data to the developer
  • The app does not access or read your email
  • All exports are user‑initiated

4. No Analytics, Tracking, or Crash Logging

The app does not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Profiling or tracking technologies
  • Network logging
  • Device fingerprinting

The app does not collect device identifiers or technical metadata.

5. Third‑Party Services

The app may request transit information from public transit APIs (such as OneBusAway or regional GTFS‑realtime services). These services receive only the stop or route information needed to return transit data. Your location and user‑entered data are not sent to these services.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with advertisers, analytics providers, third‑party companies, or government agencies. No data is sold or monetized.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users 13 and older. It does not target children and does not collect personal information from children.

8. Security

Because all user‑entered data and backups are stored locally on your device and never transmitted automatically, you maintain full control over your information. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.
